Avocado Fries

Description

These simple breaded avocados fries are just delightful! They are best served warm immediately after preparation. Enjoy!!

Ingredients

  • Avocado(s)
  • Egg(s)
  • Panko
  • Oil for frying (peanut)
  • Salt
  • Tamarind Mayo (optional sauce for serving)

Steps

  1. Cut avocado(s) into slivers somewhere around a half-inch thick.
  2. Crack an egg into a shallow bowl and beat it.
  3. Put panko in another shallow bowl.
  4. Bread the avocado slices.
    • First, dunk them in the egg.
    • Then, dunk them in the panko.
  5. Heat a generous amount of oil in the pan; about 1/4-inch deep should do the trick!
  6. Fry the avocado fries in batches until golden brown. Place them on a plate with a paper towel and salt as desired.
  7. Enjoy them warm off the griddle! They are great by themselves or with a dipping sauce like tamarind mayo.
